The 75A 4-in-1 ESC is a review-stage FPV power-electronics hardware project.

## Useful Support Topics

- High-current routing, copper area, and thermal review.
- MOSFET, gate-driver, bootstrap, and switching-node placement review.
- DSHOT signal integrity and connector documentation.
- Conservative bench-test notes before applying high current.

For power-stage review, include board images, schematic references, current
limit, load type, supply voltage, cooling setup, and any thermal observations.
