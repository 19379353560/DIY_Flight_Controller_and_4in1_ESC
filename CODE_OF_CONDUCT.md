# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes practical, technical feedback from hardware reviewers,
FPV builders, and power-electronics engineers.

## Expected

- Be specific and constructive.
- Call out safety risks clearly.
- Include schematic, PCB, measurement, or comparable-design references where
  possible.
- Keep review focused on making the design safer and easier to evaluate.

## Not Welcome

- Personal attacks, harassment, or insults.
- Unsafe advice presented as proven.
- Spam, fake engagement, or unrelated promotion.

Moderation decisions are made to keep review focused, safe, and useful.
